Our Story
About SmartMart

SmartMart was born from a simple but powerful idea — why should people in Aurangabad have to travel to big cities or rely on expensive online deliveries just to get their favourite imported products?

Founded by Shaikh Maaz & Shaikh Talha, SmartMart started as a small venture in the heart of Himayat Bagh and quickly became the go-to destination for NRIs, food enthusiasts, and anyone craving authentic imported goods right here in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar.

We don't just stock imported products — we curate them. Every item on our shelf has been chosen because it's genuinely loved — by NRIs missing home, by foodies discovering the world, and by families looking for something beyond the ordinary.

Today we carry 200+ imported products from Japan, Korea, Saudi Arabia, Indonesia and beyond. New stock arrives every week — and this website tells you exactly what's available before you make the trip.
